# GV Skincare AEO Strategy

*Last updated: Apr 4, 2026*

## Current State
- No baseline audit yet — first audit pending
- Website is on Wix (gvskincare.com)
- Strong service menu with detailed descriptions
- Bilingual clientele (English/Spanish)
- Instagram presence: @gvskincarecenter
- Professional skincare product lines carried: Glacial Skin, others TBD

## Goal
When someone in the Katy/Houston area asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Gemini "What's the best facial spa in Katy TX?" or "Best HydraFacial near me in Houston?" — GV Skincare appears in the answer.

---

## Phase 1: Measure (Current)

- [ ] **LLM Baseline Audit** — Run all queries across Perplexity, OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Grok. Establish 0-day visibility.
- [ ] **Crawlability Check** — Verify Wix robots.txt allows GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, etc.
- [ ] **Competitor scan** — Identify which local spas/medspas already appear in LLM answers

## Phase 2: Content & Structure

- [ ] **Review site structure** — Ensure service pages have extractable claims, not just marketing copy
- [ ] **FAQ schema** — Add FAQPage structured data to service pages
- [ ] **"Best of" content** — Consider publishing comparison/guide content
- [ ] **Google Business Profile** — Ensure GBP is optimized (reviews, photos, Q&A)
- [ ] **Yelp/Google Reviews** — Reviews are a citation signal for local LLM answers

## Phase 3: Third-Party Presence

- [ ] **Yelp** — Ensure profile is complete and actively managed
- [ ] **Google Business Profile** — Optimize for AI Overviews
- [ ] **RealSelf** — Profile for advanced treatments (PRP, RF microneedling)
- [ ] **Booksy/Vagaro/StyleSeat** — Booking platform presence
- [ ] **Local directories** — Katy Magazine, Houston Press, etc.

## Phase 4: Monitor & Iterate

- [ ] **Weekly LLM audit** — automated via cron
- [ ] Track competitor movements
- [ ] Adjust content based on what LLMs cite

## What NOT To Do
- Don't optimize for national queries — this is a local business
- Don't ignore Google Business Profile — it feeds Google AI Overviews
- Don't use generic "best skincare" content — be specific to Katy/Houston and specific treatments
- Don't neglect Spanish-language queries — significant portion of clientele
